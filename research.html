Research

Hydrological Responses and Compound Extremes

I study how extreme climate events affect watershed water cycles, water resource anomalies and land-surface hydrological processes under global change.

Multi-Source Earth Observation and Climate Data

My work evaluates satellite precipitation products, reanalysis datasets, CMIP6 multi-model outputs and gauge observations, with attention to bias correction, statistical downscaling and uncertainty attribution.

Climate Indices and Dataset Construction

I build climate-extreme indices and river-basin-scale climate datasets for historical and future scenarios, supporting hydrological risk assessment and carbon-water-energy cycle studies.

Large-Scale Gridded Data Analysis

I use Python and Java for NetCDF, GeoTIFF, station, remote sensing and reanalysis data processing, multi-metric diagnosis, spatial statistics, machine learning and scientific visualization.

流域水文响应与复合极端事件

关注全球变化背景下极端气候事件对水分循环、水资源异常和陆面水文过程的影响。

多源地球观测与气候数据评估

长期开展卫星降水产品、再分析数据、CMIP6 多模式输出和站点观测资料的质量评估、偏差订正、统计降尺度与不确定性分析。

气候极端指数与数据集构建

围绕历史与未来情景下的极端气候指数、流域尺度气候数据集和地球系统数据产品开展研究。

大规模格点数据科学计算

使用 Python 和 Java 处理 NetCDF、GeoTIFF、站点观测、遥感/再分析等多源时空数据，开展多指标诊断、空间统计、机器学习建模和科研绘图。
