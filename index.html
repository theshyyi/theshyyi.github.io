About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate working at the intersection of hydrology, climate extremes, remote sensing and multi-source Earth observation data assessment. My research focuses on watershed hydrological responses under global change, compound extreme events, precipitation product evaluation, CMIP6 scenario data, statistical downscaling and uncertainty diagnosis.

I have experience in building climate and hydrometeorological datasets, evaluating satellite and reanalysis precipitation products, diagnosing extreme climate indices, and processing large-scale gridded data with Python and Java.

Research Interests

  • Watershed hydrological responses under global change
  • Compound climate extremes and hydrometeorological risk
  • Satellite, reanalysis and climate model data evaluation
  • Statistical downscaling, bias correction and uncertainty analysis
  • Climate datasets, extreme indices and Earth system data products
